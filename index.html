What you get

Clear signals, concise context, and decision-ready insights — without drowning in noise.

Who it's for

Investors and traders who want faster, more confident decisions based on data and evidence.

Product

Many investment opportunities go unnoticed because people don’t monitor the full spectrum of signals — and don’t have the time or tools to combine them. LeuvBot brings indicators, fundamentals, and news into a single decision layer, turning complex information into simple, actionable insights.

How we do it

We combine statistical modeling with multiple AI agents that continuously analyze market behavior, learn from new data, and improve over time.

How it’s built

An object-oriented system where each action is explicit and traceable — designed to know when a position should be entered, held, or exited.

Vision

We believe stock market opportunities should be accessible to everyone — not only to those who rely on large banks or expensive tools. Technology can empower individuals to make better decisions and grow their money on their own terms.

Our long-term vision is to build an intelligent investing co-pilot that improves as markets evolve, through the intensive use of AI agents and advanced statistics — always focused on clarity, accountability, and actionability.
